Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Introduction

Relevant Wine ("we", "our", or "us") develops and publishes mobile games for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our applications, including Sliding Penguins and any future titles.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal data such as names or email addresses. However, our games use third-party services for advertising and analytics that may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • Usage data (app interactions, session duration, crash logs)
  • IP address (which may be used to estimate general location)

This data is collected and processed by our third-party partners as described below.

Third-Party Services

Our games use the following third-party services that may collect and process data:

  • Google Play Store — app distribution and licensing. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — in-app advertising. AdMob may use advertising identifiers and device data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Google Ads Policy
  • Google Analytics for Firebase — usage analytics and crash reporting. Firebase collects device and usage data to help us understand how our games are used and improve them. Firebase Privacy
  • Google Play Billing — in-app purchases. All transactions are processed securely by Google. We do not collect or store any payment or financial information. Google Payments Privacy

These services may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies for full details on data handling.

Children's Privacy

Our games do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through one of our third-party services, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device. Data collected by third-party services is handled according to their respective security practices and policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
